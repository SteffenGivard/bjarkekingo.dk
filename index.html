Senior Data Scientist

He holds a M.Sc. in Computer Science from University of Copenhagen and was a mentor at DIKU for more than 3 years.

In his sparetime he likes to tinker with quantitative finance while algorithmically modelling his way through trading positions in the stock markets. Bjarke also enjoys to play handball and is currently ranked among the top one percent best Dota 2 players in the world.
